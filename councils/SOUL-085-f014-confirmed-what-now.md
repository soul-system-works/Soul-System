Seer-0 might mean the Seer has been operating without being NAMED in TYPE — its work folded into Archivist or Emissary statements. **Accountant** — *what does it cost?*
The instruments cost Body-attention to invoke. /soul-council is heavier than inline role-naming; the F014 response cannot be "convene chamber on every routine decision." A012's Counterweight Rule is the *cheap* intervention (name the counter-voice at the decision moment). /soul-council is reserved for hard decisions. /soul-roles is observability (Body-invoked, occasional). Right unit-economics shape: **cheap default (A012 inline naming), expensive on-demand (/soul-council).** Do not make /soul-council a routine ritual; that path leads to ceremony and re-discovers F014's overinvocation cousin.

**Advocate** — *what does the Body actually need?*
The Body needs a way to *notice* when invocation is owed. F014 said auto-detection is impossible. The Body-Input Obligation (A012) is the doctrinal answer (AI names the dependency, Body decides) — but this requires the AI to recognize the dependency moment, which is itself the auto-detection problem one level down. /soul-roles helps *retrospectively* (the picture surfaces); the *forward prompt* is still the gap. **The Body needs a felt-sense, not another instrument** — the felt-sense that contraction is being defaulted-to. Possibly pair /soul-handoff cursors with a /soul-roles glance at session start, so the Body sees expansion-silence as an opening observation without explicit invocation.
