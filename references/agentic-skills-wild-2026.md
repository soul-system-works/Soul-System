A 2026 benchmark of how well agents actually retrieve and use skills from large collections
in realistic settings. Load-bearing for I017's central risk: ungoverned skill proliferation
**degrades selection** — so auto-generating more skills can make things worse unless
promotion + curation/retirement are first-class. Independently corroborates the Soul's own
never-always-on / RAG-MCP concern (SOUL-033). VALIDITY BASIS (F028): the paper and its
thesis are verified against the arXiv abstract; the specific magnitudes (the ~49% etc.) are
secondary-reading from the research subagent and NOT yet primary-confirmed — verify against
the full PDF before citing the numbers as fact.
