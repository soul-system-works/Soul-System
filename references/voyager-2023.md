The landmark "agent grows a skill library" result: an LLM agent in Minecraft that writes
skills as **executable code**, stores and retrieves them by similarity, and composes them
into harder behaviours — interpretable, reusable, compositional. Grounds the I017 design
pattern *generate-by-doing → verify → store → reuse*, and the claim that a durable,
code-shaped skill artifact is a proven idea (2023), not speculative. Title/authors/date
verified against the arXiv abstract; full text not read.
