The theory frame for agent memory: long-term memory splits into **episodic** (experience),
**semantic** (knowledge), and **procedural** (code & know-how). I017 is squarely
*procedural* memory, and its "float up with refinement" maps onto episodic→procedural
consolidation. Grounds the choice to reuse this established vocabulary rather than invent a
new taxonomy. Title/authors/date verified against the arXiv abstract; full text not read.
